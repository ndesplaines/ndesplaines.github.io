What Grocery Scout does

Grocery Scout helps you build grocery lists, compare store pricing, and plan the most cost-efficient trip. Location access is optional and only used with permission for nearby store discovery and location-based comparisons.

About Grocery Scout

Grocery Scout is a grocery shopping assistant designed to streamline planning, list building, store discovery, and price comparisons. The app helps users identify a cost-efficient shopping trip based on nearby stores and user-entered grocery lists.

Location access is optional. If enabled, it is used to improve nearby store discovery and provide more relevant comparisons.

Questions? Email support@njdtech.org.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 31, 2024

Grocery Scout (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use the Grocery Scout mobile application (the “App”).

Information We Collect

  • Account information (such as name and email) if you choose to sign in with Apple or Google (when enabled).
  • User-provided data such as grocery lists, preferences, and favorites you enter into the App.
  • Usage and device information such as feature interactions, diagnostic data, and device details for reliability and troubleshooting.
  • Location data only if you grant permission (used for nearby store discovery and location-based comparisons).
  • Support communications you send to support@njdtech.org.

Location Information

Location access is optional. If enabled, the App may use your approximate or precise location (depending on your device settings) to show nearby stores and to support location-based pricing comparisons. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, maintain, and improve the App and its features.

  • To respond to customer support requests.
  • To comply with applicable laws and enforce our policies.

Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may disclose information only when necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect the rights and safety of users, prevent fraud or abuse, or maintain the security and integrity of the App.

Data Retention
