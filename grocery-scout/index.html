About Grocery Scout

Grocery Scout is a grocery shopping assistant designed to streamline meal planning, grocery list building, store discovery, and price comparisons. Location access is optional and only used with permission to provide nearby store recommendations and location-based comparisons.

Questions? Email support@njdtech.org.
