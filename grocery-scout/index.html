Grocery Scout helps you organize grocery lists, compare prices, and discover nearby stores. Location is used only with permission for nearby recommendations and location-based comparisons.

About Grocery Scout

Grocery Scout is a grocery shopping assistant designed to streamline meal planning, grocery list building, store discovery, and price comparisons. The app may use optional location access (with permission) to provide nearby store recommendations and location-based price comparisons.
