Privacy Policy (summary)

Grocery Scout may collect account information (name/email via Google/Apple), profile preferences, grocery lists/history, price comparison searches, and device/usage info. Location data is collected only with permission for nearby recommendations and location-based comparisons.

To request access, deletion, or export of your data, contact support@njdtech.org.
