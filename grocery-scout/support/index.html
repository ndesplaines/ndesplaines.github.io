FAQ: Why do prices differ from what I see in-store?

Prices can change and vary by location, availability, promotions, and retailer updates. Always verify with the retailer before purchasing.

FAQ: Location access — is it required?

No. Location is used only with permission to improve nearby store recommendations and location-based comparisons.

FAQ: How do I stop notifications?

Disable notifications in iOS Settings → Notifications → Grocery Scout, and/or in-app preferences if available.
