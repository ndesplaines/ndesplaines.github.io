FAQ: Why are prices different than in-store?

Prices can vary by location, availability, promotions, and retailer updates. Always verify prices with the retailer before purchasing.

FAQ: Is location required?

No. Location is optional and used only with permission for nearby store discovery and location-based comparisons.

FAQ: How do I disable notifications?

Disable notifications in iOS Settings → Notifications → Grocery Scout (and/or in-app preferences if available).
