FAQ: Is Prophecy Sports a sportsbook?

No. Prophecy Sports is informational/entertainment only. We do not accept bets, process wagers, or operate as a sportsbook.

FAQ: My odds/picks look off — what should I send support?

Send the league, matchup, time (with timezone), sportsbook shown, and a screenshot. If possible, include your region/state because odds feeds can vary.

FAQ: How do I request account deletion?

Email support@njdtech.org from the email associated with your account and request deletion (or use in-app deletion if available).
